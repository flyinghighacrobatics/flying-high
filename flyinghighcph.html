Flying High Copenhagen 7-8 November 2015


3 Workshops, "on the road to standing Hand to Hand!"

Some level of acro experience required - you should be familiar with the *prerequisites

Sat 11:00 - 13:00

Handstand training, learn prep drills that will prepare you for h2h, and solidify the foundations of standing acro
*can hold a handstand for 30+ seconds with a spotter or wall

Sat 15:00 -18:00

Starting with variations on h2h with a laying base and work our way up to standing, exploring high transitions and finally standing h2h
* flyers: kick into a handstand on the ground and turn out assisted
bases: foot to hand

Sun 09:00 - 11:00

Pops into h2h on a laying base and jumping into standing h2h...we will intelligently use progressions and tempo drills to get you more calibrated and comfortable flying high!
* only open to those who attended the Sat 3-6 session
